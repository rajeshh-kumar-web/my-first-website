Uttarakhand Nursing & Medical Admission 2026 🩺

Last Updated: March 2026 | By UK Mega Portal Guidance Team

Uttarakhand mein B.Sc Nursing, GNM, aur ANM mein admission lene ke liye HNBUMU (Medical Education University) dwara entrance exam karwaye jate hain. Is saal ki poori jankari niche di gayi hai.

✅ Important Note: Nursing entrance exams July-August 2026 mein hone ki umeed hai. Taiyari ke liye Physics, Chemistry aur Biology (NCERT) par focus karein.

Eligibility Criteria (Kaun Apply Kar Sakta Hai?)

  • B.Sc Nursing: 12th Pass (Physics, Chemistry, Biology) with 45% marks.
  • GNM: 12th Pass kisi bhi stream se (40% marks).
  • ANM: Sirf female candidates ke liye, 10+2 pass.
