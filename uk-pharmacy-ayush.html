Uttarakhand Pharmacy & AYUSH Admission Guide 2026 💊🌿

Update: March 2026 | Sahi Jankari, Sahi Career - UK Mega Portal

Uttarakhand mein Pharmacy (B.Pharma/D.Pharma) aur AYUSH (BAMS/BHMS) courses mein admission lene ke liye har saal hazaron bache apply karte hain. Iska poora admission process niche samjhaya gaya hai.

📢 Latest Update: B.Pharma admission ke liye UKSEE (Uttarakhand State Entrance Exam) ya CUET ke marks zaroori ho sakte hain. AYUSH (BAMS) ke liye NEET qualify karna compulsory hai.

Pharmacy Courses (Eligibility & Scope)

  • D.Pharma (2 Years): 12th Pass (PCB/PCM). Ye Diploma course hai medical store kholne ke liye best hai.
  • B.Pharma (4 Years): 12th Pass (PCB/PCM). Degree course jo pharmaceutical companies aur drug inspector banne ke liye zaroori hai.

AYUSH Courses (BAMS/BHMS/BUMS)

Uttarakhand Ayurveda University (UAU) ke antargat aane wale colleges mein BAMS/BHMS ke liye **NEET Entrance Exam** dena hota hai. Counseling Uttarakhand Ayush Counseling board dwara karwayi jati hai.
